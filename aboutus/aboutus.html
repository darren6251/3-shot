Get to know Us.

3-Shot is a video editing company founded by three high school students. Our aim is to create custom videos that are trendy and appealing to all, from the perspective of young generations.

At 3-Shot, we firmly believe in the accessibility of video creation, adhering to the mantra "Take, Edit, Export! Creating video has never been easier than this before."
